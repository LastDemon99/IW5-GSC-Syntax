Name: Objective_Team
Function: Objective_Team( <objective_number>, <team> )
Module: Objective
Restriction: MP Only
Summary: Sets the team that the objective is for. Allows having different objectives for each team
Example: Objective_Team( 0, "allies" );
RequiredArgs:
1 : <objective_number> The ID of the objective to alter
2 : <team> The team that the objective is for. Valid entries are 'allies', 'axis', or 'none'
