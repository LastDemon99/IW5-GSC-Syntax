Name: SetPhysicsGravityDir
Function: SetPhysicsGravityDir( <direction> )
Module: Physics
Restriction: SP Only
Summary: Sets the direction of gravity for physics.
Example: SetPhysicsGravityDir( (0,0,1) );
RequiredArgs:
1 : <direction> The direction that gravity will pull (i.e., "down").  Set to (0,0,-1) for normal gravity behavior.
