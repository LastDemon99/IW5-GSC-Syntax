Name: SetWeaponSlotWeapon
Function: SetWeaponSlotWeapon( <weapon slot> )
Module: Player
Summary: Sets the the weapon in the given weapon slot.
CallOn: The player
Example: level.player SetWeaponSlotWeapon("primary", weapon);
RequiredArgs:
1 : <weapon slot>: Valid weaponslots are "primary" and "primaryb".
2 : <weapon name>: The name of the weapon to use in this slot.
