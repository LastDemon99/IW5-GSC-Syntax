Name: Target_SetAttackMode
Function: Target_SetAttackMode( <target entity>, <mode name> )
Module: Objective
Restriction: SP Only
Summary: Sets how missiles and rockets should approach the target.
Example: Target_SetAttackMode( enemy_tank, "top" );
RequiredArgs:
1 : <target entity> The entity that is the target
2 : <mode name> "top" or "direct"
