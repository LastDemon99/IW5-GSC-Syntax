Name: ViewKick
Function: ViewKick( <force>, <source> )
Module: Player
Summary: Damage the player, and throw the screen around
CallOn: <player>
Example: level.player ViewKick( 127, level.player.origin );
RequiredArgs:
1 : <force> The force of the kick, from 0 to 127
2 : <source> (a point) the source of the kick
