Name: getweaponslotammo
Function: getweaponslotammo( <weapon slot> )
Module: Player

CallOn: The player
Example: ammo = level.player getweaponslotammo("primary");
RequiredArgs:
1 : <weapon slot>: Valid weaponslots are "primary" and "primaryb".
