Name: GetWeaponSlotClipAmmo
Function: GetWeaponSlotClipAmmo( <weapon slot> )
Module: Player
Summary: Gets the ammunition in the clip for the weapon in the given weapon slot.
CallOn: The player
Example:  ammo = level.player GetWeaponSlotClipAmmo("primary");
RequiredArgs:
1 : <weapon slot>: Valid weaponslots are "primary" and "primaryb".
