Name: getweaponslotweapon
Function: getweaponslotweapon( <weapon slot> )
Module: Player
Summary: Gets the name of the weapon in the given weapon slot.
CallOn: The player
Example: playerWeapon[0] = level.player getweaponslotweapon("primary");
RequiredArgs:
1 : <weapon slot>: Valid weaponslots are "primary" and "primaryb".
