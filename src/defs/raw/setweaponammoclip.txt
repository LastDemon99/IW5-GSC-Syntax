Name: SetWeaponAmmoClip
Function: SetWeaponAmmoClip( <weapon name>, <ammunition> )
Module: Player
Summary: Set the weapon clip ammunition for the given weapon.
CallOn: The player
Example:  level.player SetWeaponAmmoClip( "m16", 30 );
RequiredArgs:
1 : <weapon name>: (string) The weapon name for this weapon.
2 : <ammunition>: (integer) The amount of ammunition in the clip.
