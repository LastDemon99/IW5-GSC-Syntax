Name: setweaponslotclipammo
Function: setweaponslotclipammo( <weapon slot>, <ammo count> )
Module: Player
Summary: Sets the clip ammunition for the weapon in the given weapon slot.
CallOn: The player
Example:  level.player setweaponslotclipammo("primary", 125);
RequiredArgs:
1 : <weapon slot>: Valid weaponslots are "primary" and "primaryb".
2 : <ammo count>: The amount of ammunition.
