Name: Target_SetJavelinOnly
Function: Target_SetJavelinOnly( <target entity>, <true/false> )
Module: Objective
Restriction: SP Only
Summary: Target will only draw on player's hud when they are looking through the Javelin-weapon's site.
Example: Target_SetJavelinOnly( enemy_tank, true );
RequiredArgs:
1 : <target entity> The entity that is the target
2 : <mode name> "top" or "direct"
