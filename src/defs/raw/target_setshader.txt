Name: Target_SetShader
Function: Target_SetShader( <target entity>, <materialname> )
Module: Objective
Restriction: SP Only
Summary: Changes the material of a target.  The target must have already been created with target_set()
Example: Target_SetShader( enemy_chopper, "locked_on_shader" );
RequiredArgs:
1 : <target entity> The entity that is the target
2 : <materialname> The shader for the quad drawn over the target
