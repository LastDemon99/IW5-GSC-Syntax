Name: Vibrate
Function: Vibrate( <direction vector>, <amplitude>, <period>, <time> )
Module: Motion
Restriction: SP Only
Summary: Causes a script entity to vibrate, rotating around its origin, along a given vector dir
Example: self Vibrate( directionVir, 0.3, 0.4, 1.0 );
RequiredArgs:
1 : <direction vector> The direction of the vibration
2 : <amplitude> The amount of the vibration in degrees
3 : <period> The period of the vibration in seconds
4 : <time> The length of time of the vibration in seconds
